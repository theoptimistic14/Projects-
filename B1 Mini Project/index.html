Eligiblity Criteria

Age

Donor should be in the age group of 18 to 65 years.

Weight

The Donor Should not be less than 45 Kilograms.

Infection

Should NOT have Hepatitis B, C, Tuberculosis, Leprosy, HIV.

Vaccination

Should NOT had shots for any of the following - COVID-19, Cholera, Typhoid, Diptheria, Tetanus, Plague, Gammaglobulin in last 30 days; Rabies vaccination in last 1 year.

Disease

Should NOT have Epilepsy, Asthma on Steroids, Bleeding disorders, Thalassemia, Sickle Cell Anemia, Polycythemia Vera.

Tattoo & Scars

Should NOT have had Tattoo in last 6 months.Arms & Forearms should be free from Skin punctures or Scars which are indicative of Intravenous drug use or frequent blood donation.

DONATION PROCESS

1. REGISTRATION

You need to complete a very simple registration form which contatins all
equired contact information to enter in the donation process.

2. SCREENING

A drop of blood from your finger will be taken for a simple test to ensure
that your blood levels are proper enough for donation.

3. DONATION

After passing screening test successfully you will be directed
to a donor bed for donation. It will take only 6-10 minutes.

4. REFRESHMENT

you can also stay in the sitting room until you feel strong enough to leave the center.
You will receive refreshments in the donation zone
